My Favourite Anime List

TOP 5 ANIMES

1. Naruto

Description:

Naruto tells the story of Naruto Uzumaki, a young ninja who dreams of becoming the Hokage of his village.

2. One Piece

Description:

One Piece follows Monkey D. Luffy and his pirate crew searching for the legendary treasure called the One Piece.

3. Demon Slayer: Infinity Castle

Description:

Demon Slayer follows Tanjiro Kamado, a boy who becomes a demon slayer after demons destroy his family.

4. Solo Leveling

Description:

Solo Leveling tells the story of Sung Jin-Woo, the weakest hunter who gains the power to level up and become the strongest.

5. Attack on Titan

Description:

Attack on Titan is about humanity fighting giant creatures called Titans. The story follows Eren Yeager and his friends.
